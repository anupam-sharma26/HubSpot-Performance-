# HubSpot-Performance-
This dashboard shows IT pipeline performance with deal counts, win/loss rates, revenue, and stage distribution. It highlights trends in incoming leads, stage movements, and deal ownership, revealing concentration in services, industries, and creators.

This ITK Pipeline dashboard provides a full snapshot of sales performance and deal flow.

At the top, key KPIs summarize business health:

Active Deals (4546): Total open opportunities currently in the pipeline.

Closed Lost (171): Deals that did not convert.

Closed Won (646): Successfully converted deals.

Win Rate (10.73%) and Loss Rate (2.84%) show conversion efficiency.

Won Amount (377.00K) and Active Amount (685.60M) represent revenue performance.

Distribution Insights:

Deals by Service Type: Dominated by a single category (~93%), indicating heavy concentration in one service offering.

Deals by Stage: Most deals are in Incoming Leads (46.1%) and Prospect (36.8%), suggesting early-stage pipeline weighting.

Deals by Industry: One industry contributes ~97% of deals, showing limited diversification.

Deals by Creator: Majority of deals are attributed to a single creator (~92.7%), highlighting dependency on one source.

Trend Analysis:

Stage Movement Chart: Shows how deals move across stages over weeks in 2025–2026.

Incoming Leads Trend: Displays weekly lead inflow, with a sharp spike around week 44.

Non-Unique Stage Changes: Tracks repeated movements across stages, indicating possible pipeline churn or inefficiencies.

Overall, the dashboard emphasizes pipeline volume, stage distribution, ownership concentration, and temporal trends.
